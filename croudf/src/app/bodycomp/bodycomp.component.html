What Is Crowdfunding?

Crowdfunding is a method of raising funds for a project or venture by collecting small contributions from a large number of people, typically via the internet. It allows individuals or businesses to present their idea or project to a wide audience and invite them to contribute financially in exchange for rewards or early access. This approach democratizes funding, enabling projects that may not have access to traditional financing to get off the ground with support from the community.
